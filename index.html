✦ The Lore ✦

What is Hackwarts?

Born at the crossroads of ancient wizardry and cutting-edge technology, Hackwarts is not just a hackathon — it is an ordeal.

Teams of brilliant minds step into four legendary Houses of Challenge, each demanding mastery over a different domain of magic: data, design, deployment, and disruption. Through a relentless Hack Marathon, competitors forge solutions that matter — powered by caffeine, creativity, and a dash of dark magic.

Mentored by industry Headmasters, judged by a council of Archmages, and witnessed by hundreds of spellbound spectators — Hackwarts crowns only those who dare.
